### What is my project about?

This week marked the official start of my In2Research internship! I am working on a project titled “Strengthening healthcare resilience to climate extremes: a spatial modelling framework.” As climate change accelerates, threats like intense flooding and heatwave-driven power cuts present risks to NHS infrastructure. My project is part of a larger collaboration with the NHS Blood and Transplant (NHSBT), which builds spatio-temporal agent-based models to simulate how climate events disrupt the national supply of blood.


### Overall Goal

My specific focus is to use Python and R to find the underlying factors driving operational costs and vulnerabilities across NHS sites when extreme weather hits. Over the next 8 weeks, I will be building two distinct predictive frameworks: Machine Learning approaches (Gradient Boosting) and Bayesian modelling to map these climate risks and handle data uncertainty.
